JET SOLUTIONS PROVIDER LTD

Jet Solutions Provider Ltd is a service company and brings office solutions to our valued customers. We are specialized in: Printers & Scanners: Océ - Canon - Epson - Wide Tek - Wide Format Printing, Scanners, Consumables and Parts - Monochrome and Colours.

OCÉ - CANON: Large Format Printer Color and monochrome. EPSON: Printers A4 /A3 – Dot Matrix Printer and Wide Format. WIDE TEK: Wide Format Scanners, Monochrome and Colors. COLORTRAC: Wide Format Scanners, Monochrome and Colors. HP: Inkjet Laserjet, Designjet and Scanners.

Other brands: FLEXA, MUTOH, SUMMA, JETS-PRO UPS SYSTEM, IMAGE ACCESS, INFOSEC, BOOKEYE 4, EVOLIS, DELL, APPLE


Jet Solutions Provider Ltd has its head office in Mauritius and we work with different partners actually in the Indian Ocean Area and is made up of dynamic and experienced persons. Our technicians are certified and their skills are updated continuously so as to perform maintenance and installation for ICT and machines. Our efficient after-sales services enable us to keep our customers satisfied. Finally, we work daily to give the best quality products and services to satisfy our customers and partners.
